Final Fantasy XV

Review by: Carlos Arce - Spring 2019 | Game Publisher: Square Enix | Console: PC - Xbox - PS4 | Game Released: November 29, 2016

Prince Noctis

Final Fantasy 15 – Developer Square | Enix PC – Xbobx – PS4 Final Fantasy 15 is an action rpg. The story takes place in the world of Eos, a world full of fantasy creatures and magic. Prince Noctis and his friends to go on a journey to save the world of Eos from a mysterious threat.

Game Review - Final Fantasy XV

  • Characters:
    • Noctis Lucis Caelum
    • Ignis Scientia
    • Gladiolus Amicitia
    • Prompto Argentum

Noctis, Prince and protagonist of this game. He uses weapons known as King Glaives that grant him special abilities.

Ignis, also body guard to the king. He serves the role of healer for allies and status ailment inflictor to enemies during combat. He is also the self-proclaimed chef of the crew.

Gladiolus, personal body guard to Noct since the prince was a child. He serves the role of tank during combat, often times delivering heavy blows that stun enemies.

Prompto, childhood friend of Noct who serves as ranged damage dealer during combat. He is provides comedic relief many times, and he is the self-proclaimed photographer of the game.

The game includes a magic crafting mechanic, which allows you to use magic during combat. Use magic to change the tide of combat. Most enemies have an elemental weakness. Use magic to punish difficult enemies during combat.

Magic Use - Final Fantasy XV

There are also guardian deities, called Summons, across the world of Eos. Find them to gain use of them, but beware that you will have to battle them to unlock their use during battle.

  • Summons:
    • Titan
    • Ramuh
    • Shiva
    • Bahamut

Progress through the main story to unlock all Summons

Summons - Final Fantasy XV

Enjoy navigating off the marked path to find challenging bosses and hidden treasures. In this action-rpg style Final Fantasy game.
