Dragon Ball Fighter Z

Review by: Carlos Arce - Spring 2019 | Game Publisher: Bandai Namco | Console: PC - Xbox - PS4 | Game Released: January 26, 2018

Super Sayan Goku - Dragon Ball Fighter Z

Bandai Namco joined with Arc System Works to create a Dragon Ball fighting game like no other. This games feels like a revamped classic fighting game, similar to Street Fighter.

Game Review - Dragon Ball Fighter Z

Dragon Ball Fighter Z features all of the original Dragon Ball characters, and it features new characters created solely for this game. All characters have their own unique specials, move sets, and super finishers.

  • Returning Characters:
    • Goku
    • Gohan
    • Vegeta
    • Trunks
    • Krillin
    • Yamcha
    • Android 17
    • Android 18

The game also features a wide range new characters to the series.

  • New Characters:
    • Hit
    • Beerus
    • Android 21
    • Zamasu
    • Jiren

Super Finishers - Dragon Ball Figher Z

All of these characters offer different combat abilities. Practice with different characters to chose your favorites.
