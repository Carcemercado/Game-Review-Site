Legend of Zelda - Breath of the Wild

Review by: Carlos Arce - Spring 2019 | Game Publisher: Nintendo Entertainment | Console: Switch | Game Released: January 26, 2018

Nintendo has released a great action-adventure game. The game focuses on Link's journey across the Kingdom of Hyrule. Link is on a mission to defend Hyrule from an threat known as Ganon.

Game Review - Legend of Zelda B.O.T.W.

You uncover many weapons and magical runes that will help you through your adventure. As Link travels through Hyrule, he discovers memories from his past. This leads him to seek help from the Hyrulian races. Link must obtain the Master Sword from the Lost Woods in order to enter Hyrule Castle and defeat Ganon.

Races and Tribes - Legend of Zelda B.O.T.W.

Gerudo,they populate the deserts of Hyrule and the shores of Termina

Goron, they populate mountainous areas, whether snowy or fiery. They are incredibly durable, able to resist fire and lava.

Hylian, they were the first race to establish organized civilization in Hyrule, and as such they populate the largest and most powerful countries.

Kokiri, they live in the Kokiri Forest under the care of their guardian fairies and the Great Deku Tree, never to grow up.

Rito, they have the ability to fly makes them invaluable for communications. They are not born with wings; they acquire these later in life.

Sheikah, they are close cousins of the Hylians. They are a secretive, magical race, they are devoted to serve the Three Goddesses and the Royal Family of Hyrule.

Twili, they were group of dark sorcerers who used “old magic” known as the Fused Shadows. They were banished to the Twilight Realm after attempting to take the Triforce. They eventually adapted to their new surroundings and established a new culture in the Twilight Realm.

Zora, they are a dolphin-like race that tend Hyrule’s rivers and seas. They are ruled by a monarch and are the only known race to hatch out of eggs.

Tips and Tricks - Legend of Zelda B.O.T.W.

Take your time exploring Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild. There are incredible weapons, secret dungeons, and NPC characters all over Hyrule.
