Hollow Knight

Review by: Carlos Arce - Spring 2019 | Game Publisher: Team Cherry | Console: PC - Xbox - PS4 - Switch | Game Released: February 24, 2017

Knight - Hollow Knight

In this beautiful Metroidvania style game, you play as a character known as the Ghost. The Ghost is a knight that travels a long-abandoned bug kingdom known as Hallownest. Within this kingdom, you will find challenging monsters, ancient treasures, unique companions, and special abilities.

Game Review - Hollow Knight

As you journey deeper into Hallownest, you gain new abilities through exploration. In Hollow Knight, curiosity is often times rewarded. This games is the most expansive game in the series, as it features 12 locations to explore:

  • Map Locations:
    • Forgotten Crossroads
    • Greenpath
    • Fungal Wastes
    • Crystal Peak
    • City of Tears
    • Deepnest
    • Royal Waterways
    • Fog Canyon
    • Resting Grounds
    • Stagway Station

See this image for a detailed map.

Hallownest Map - Hollow Knight

The knight uses a weapon known as a Nail, which you can level up as you progress through the game. Leveling up the Nail allows you to defeat enemies easier and increases your chances of survival. There are many upgrades and abilities hidden throughout the world of Hallownest. Find talismans, masks, and ancient relics to increase the Knight's survival chance.

Watch this video to see all of the Knight's abilities.

In this game curiosity is greatly rewarded. Don't be afraid to explore every nook and cranny of the map. More times than not, you are meant to wander off the beaten path in order to progress.
