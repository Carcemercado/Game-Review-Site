Tekken 7

Review by: Carlos Arce - Spring 2019 | Game Publisher: Bandai Namco | Console: PC - Xbox - PS4 | Game Released: March 18, 2015

Heihachi vs. Kazuya - Tekken 7

Classic arcade style fighting game. Tekken 7 features a wide range of playable characters, each one specializing in a martial art. The story mode is different in this game, where you must clear the story mode with different characters to unlock the complete story. This means that you have to clear the story with all characters if you want to understand the storyline.

Game Review - Tekken 7

There are many characters to choose from, each one unique in its own way. Try out classic Tekken fighters, such as:

  • Classic Characters:
    • Jin Kazama
    • Nina Williams
    • Yoshimitsu
    • Hwoarang
    • Kazuya Mishima
    • Heihachi Mishima
    • Lance
    • Gunlance
    • Switch Axe
    • Charge Blade
  • New Characters:
    • Akuma
    • Nina Williams
    • Gigas
    • Katarina Alves
    • Master Raven

These are some suggestions to start out, keep in mind that there are 50 characters to try out. All characters have simple combos as well as complex 10-hit combos. A new addition to the game is the ability to counter with a super move that could end the fight instantly; however, this is only possible when your health is almost completely depleted.

Super Moves - Tekken 7

You can test your fighting skills against other players via the Multiplayer Local and Online feature. This feature offers a ranking system that unlocks new banners based on your win ratio. The more wins you accrue, the more options for customizing your character. You can also gain decorations and outfits for your character by playing survival mode to earn in game cash, which helps cover the cost of these outfits.
