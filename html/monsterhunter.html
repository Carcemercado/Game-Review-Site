Monster Hunter World

Review by: Carlos Arce - Spring 2019 | Game Publisher: Capcom | Console: PC - Xbox - PS4 | Game Released: January 26, 2018

Nergigante - Monster Hunter World

Capcom has released a marvelous game. Monster Hunter World brings all of the excitement from the Monster Hunter series to consoles and PC. This game features a massive new world as well as new monsters exclusive to this entry, such as the Nergigante pictured above.

Game Review - Monster Hunter World

Monster Hunter world is the most expansive entry in the Monster Hunter series. This games is the most expansive game in the series, as it features 8 locations to explore. Each map offers its own unique environment with corresponding wildlife, which make Monster Hunter World feel like a complete biosphere with its own gigantic ecosystem.

  • Map Locations:
    • Astera
    • Research Base
    • Ancient Forest
    • Wild Spire Waster
    • Great Ravine
    • Coral Highlands
    • Rotten Vale
    • Elder's Recess

See this video for a detailed view of the maps.

Maps - Monster Hunter World

The game also features a wide range of weapon types that provide very different possibilities for taking down monsters.

  • Melee Weapon types:
    • Great Sword
    • Long Sword
    • Sword and Shield
    • Dual Blades
    • Hammer
    • Hunting Horn
    • Lance
    • Gunlance
    • Switch Axe
    • Charge Blade
  • Ranged Weapon types:
    • Light Bowgun
    • Heavy Bowgun
    • Bow

Weapon Types - Monster Hunter World

All of these weapons are great. Take your time and practice with any of these weapon types to chose your favorites.
